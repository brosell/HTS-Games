Overview

Playing Barrel Drop is simple. Groups of colorful barrels fall from the top of the screen and stop when they land on the ground or on another barrel.. You must try arranging the Barrels so that Barrels of the same color are touching.

When FOUR or more same color Barrels are touching they disappear and you earn points. When blocks disappear, any blocks above them in the same column fall to fill in the space.

Controls

LEFT Arrow Shift the group of Barrels to the left
RIGHT Arrow Shift the group of Barrels to the right
DOWN Arrow Nudge the group of Barrels down
UP Arrow (or left CTRL) Rotate the Barrels to the left
left ALT Rotate the Barrels to the right
P Pause
Q Quit

Skill Levels

Easy Nice and Slow. You must clear 25 Barrels to proceed to the next level
Medium A little faster. 35 Barrels are required to proceed to the next level.
Hard Faster Still. You need 50 Barrels to proceed to the next level

Game Modes

Normal

In Normal Mode, whenever you complete a level, any Barrels still on the board are removed so you start with a clear board.

Survivor

In Survivor Mode the board is not cleared between levels.

Special Barrels

There are two kinds of special barrels, WILD and RUSTY.

Up Down Wild

This Barrel always appears by itself. When placed on a stack of Barrels, every Barrel in the stack disappears and you earn points

Left Right Wild

This Barrel always appears by itself. When placed on the board, all the Barrels in the same row disappear and you earn points

Rusty

When a RUSTY Barrel appears, you’re in trouble! RUSTY Barrels don’t disappear when matched up. They can only be removed by using one of the WILD Barrels.

Bonuses

If you match more than one set of blocks in one turn, the score for the second match is double, the score for the third match  is triple, etc.

There is a BONUS of 2000 points for clearing the board of all barrels
